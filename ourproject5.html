Raddison Plaza

The Raddison Plaza expansion includes a new pool and outdoor amenities area with restaurant and bar; renovated rooms into 8 two-bay suites with new balconies; a new 15,000-sq.-ft., glass-enclosed non-smoking casino area as well as an expanded main casino floor; and a new restaurant with infinity bar. The new Tourmaline Pools feature five pools surrounded by 14 luxury cabanas, two oversized hot tubs, a poolside bar and restaurant and a lounge area with fire pits..
