Sangali Bank co

72,000-sq.-ft. ground-up construction of a three-story, warm shell medical office building. The building exterior will consist of a mixture of stone veneer and stucco with a glazed curtain wall entry tower. The site will incorporate bioswales that are strategically placed through the parking lot and site perimeter to provide natural treatment and filtration of debris from storm water on the site. This project is targeting LEED Platinum certification..

MPK 21, adjacent to MPK 20, is 524,000 sq. ft. of new office space with break-away spaces, service amenities and a 2,000-seat event space on a heavily landscaped 22.7 acre portion of the overall parcel. While MPK 20 was big and fast, MPK 21 was bigger and faster: the project is almost 100,000 sq. ft. larger but was completed in 17 months vs. 22 months.
