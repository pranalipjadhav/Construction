26th & Alcott

Encompassing a full city block in the Jefferson Park neighborhood of Denver, the project consists of 2-multifamily load bearing metal stud towers atop a 4-story partially below grade post tensioned parking garage. The first tower houses 93 short term rental units while the second tower houses 533 apartment style units, 626 units total. The 15-story highrise includes a third-floor amenity deck with an outdoor pool, yoga studio, fitness area, media and game rooms, a club room and two courtyards. Construction began in October 2021 .Construction for some time on the renovation of a large Grade II listed building which needed significant modernisation. The works have included underfloor heating, structural work, restoration of the roof

reconfiguring rooms, new bathrooms, insulation throughout, flooring, kitchen, windows and roof lights. Due to the nature of the building and often difficult phasing of the work it has been imperative to work with a company who have committed to providing a certain level of quality in terms of workmanship and delivery.
